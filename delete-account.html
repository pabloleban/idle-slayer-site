How to delete your account?

You can delete your account or data either from the game or from the web.

From the game

Open the Settings and scroll down to the bottom and you'll see the Delete Save and Account button.

From the web

Go to https://idleslayer.com/account and sign in with your account. You'll find the Delete Account button below.
